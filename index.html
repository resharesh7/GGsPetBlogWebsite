About Us

Our Story – In Honor of My Brother & Best Friend GG’s Pet Shop was born from love, loss, and the healing bond between humans and animals. I lost my brother—my best friend. He had a heart full of love for animals, especially his gentle giant, Guardian the Cane Corso. In his darkest moments, when he felt alone, Guardian was there—loyal, comforting, and unwavering. Their bond reminded me of something powerful: animals don’t just make great pets—they become family, therapists, and friends. This store is a tribute to him and every person who’s ever found peace in a wagging tail or healing in a purr. GG’s Pet Shop exists to celebrate the joy pets bring us, and to support others through sustainable products that make a difference—for our furry companions and the planet. Because pets aren’t just pets. They’re guardians of our hearts.

🦴 Top Natural Treats for Dogs

When it comes to our pups, we all want to give them the very best — especially when it comes to treats! But not all doggy snacks are created equal. Many commercial treats are packed with artificial flavors, fillers, preservatives, and ingredients we can’t even pronounce. 😬

Spoiler alert:That’s why natural treats are taking over treat jars everywhere — and your dog’s tail will be wagging for more.

But here's the good news: eco-friendly alternatives are everywhere — and many are just as affordable, safe, and stylish (if not more!) than traditional options.

♻️🦴Natural Treats for Dogs

  • 🥜 Peanut Butter Stuffed Bones
  • 🐄 All-Natural Beef Chews
  • 🐟 Dehydrated Fish Skins
  • 🍌 Homemade Fruit & Veggie Chips
  • 🐾 Limited Ingredient Biscuits

Let’s dig into the top natural dog treats your pup (and their tummy) will love:

1. 🥜 Peanut Butter Stuffed Bones Dogs go nuts for peanut butter! Especially when it's stuffed in a real bone made for chewing. These treats provide hours of entertainment, are high in protein, and help clean teeth while your pup chews. Just make sure it’s xylitol-free — xylitol is toxic to dogs. GG’s Pick: SCP Bone Peanut Butter Stuffed Chew (3–4") – natural, delicious, and safe!

Click here if you're thinking ("I need this treat for my pup")

2. 🐄 All-Natural Beef Chews Beef bully sticks, tendons, and tracheas are single-ingredient, fully digestible, and irresistible to dogs. They’re also great for promoting healthy chewing habits and reducing plaque. Eco Tip: Choose grass-fed or responsibly sourced options for a lower environmental impact.

3. 🐟 Dehydrated Fish Skins These are crunchy, high in Omega-3 fatty acids, and totally free of additives. Fish treats support a healthy coat, brain, and joints. Plus, dogs love the smell (even if you don’t 😅). Bonus: They're a great option for dogs with chicken or beef allergies.

4. 🍌 Homemade Fruit & Veggie Chips Sweet potatoes, bananas, apples (no seeds), and carrots can be dehydrated into crunchy, healthy snacks. No preservatives, no fillers — just real, natural goodness straight from your kitchen. Paw Tip: You can even freeze them in ice cubes with water for a fun summer snack!

5. 🐾 Limited Ingredient Biscuits Look for treats with fewer than five ingredients — things like oats, pumpkin, honey, and coconut oil. Natural biscuits are easier on the digestive system and better for pups with sensitive stomachs.

Pro Tip:

Always check the label. If you can't pronounce it, your dog probably shouldn't eat it.

🐕 🐶 Treat Them Right – Naturally

Giving your dog treats isn’t just about rewarding good behavior — it’s a chance to boost their health, energy, and happiness. By choosing natural options, you're saying yes to better nutrition, fewer vet visits, and a longer, tail-wagging life.
